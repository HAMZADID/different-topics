Colds

Colds are the most common infectious diseases in humans. In fact, colds appear as a result of exposure to some types of viruses that attack the upper respiratory tract. The most common virus is the Corona virus, also known as coronary or coronary virus, as well as the virus known as the nasal virus. The reason behind the prevalence of this type of health problems that there are more than two hundred virus can cause the person suffering from cold, so the body can not be resistant against all these species, as demonstrated by the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention According to her statistics conducted that adults are likely to suffer from colds two to three times per year, while the child is more susceptible to colds, it was found that they had infected 12 times per year. As for the way in which the infection is transmitted, it can be said that the arrival of the spray of the injured person to the healthy person may be sufficient for the disease, and the fact that the spray can reach by coughing or sneezing or touching the surfaces contaminated with the spray of the injured, Two days after the onset of symptoms and continue to be able to transmit the infection until the disappearance of symptoms permanently.
